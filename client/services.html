WHAT IS DIGITIZING?

Digitizing is a crucial process in the embroidery industry, as it involves transforming artwork into a digital format for embroidery machines to understand and execute. While it may not be an automated process, skilled digitizing is an art form that enables the creation of intricate designs with unique features like bars and extra stitches.
